A passion for bees About

Imagine having honey from your own backyard? More and more people want bees in their gardens and surroundings. Bees are fascinating and they can easily become an obsession, in a good way. Bees are important to our food supply, keeping bees was once simple but now it can be a real challenge just to keep a hive of bees alive.

We love keeping bees at Yep Bees Ltd. and want you to love it too. Beekeeping has become more and more popular in the past 5 years, and for good cause. New Zealand has begun to open the doors for honeybees and begun giving bees the respect they deserve as super pollinators. 


Keeping bees is much more than harvesting honey, bees connect us with our natural environment, with the weather and the seasons, and best of all they bring such pleasure. Through pollination, your honeybees will really improve your local environment, creating more flowers, bigger fruits and vegetables and healthier plants all around.
